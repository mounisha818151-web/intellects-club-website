INTELLECTS CLUB

SRM INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY, RAMAPURAM

Intellects Club is a student-driven technical community at SRM IST Ramapuram dedicated to innovation, learning, and leadership. We empower students through workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, and real-world projects to build technical and professional skills.

what we've actually done

A running log of session, workshops, and events-not a highlight reel, the real timeline.

GitHub Repository Week Challenge

A hands-on challenge that introduced students to GitHub, version control, and repository management while helping them build professional project portfolios.

Blind Stack Challenge

Participants were assigned a random technology stack and challenged to build a project, testing their creativity and adaptability.

Speed hackathon

A fast-paced hackathon where participants built innovative solutions within a limited time, enhancing teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Chatbot Alchemy

A hands-on workshop where participants learned to design, build, and deploy AI-powered chatbots through interactive sessions and practical challenges.
